The weather in England is famously unpredictable, with frequent rain throughout the 
entire year and occasional sunny periods. English people are widely known for their 
characteristic politeness, orderly queuing culture and traditional love of tea drinking. 
The education system in England consists of primary school, secondary school and various 
university levels. Many prestigious English universities like Oxford and Cambridge are 
world-renowned educational institutions. Football is extremely popular in England, with 
the Premier League being enthusiastically watched by millions globally. The royal family 
remains an important and symbolic part of English culture and national tradition. English 
gardens are famous worldwide for their beautiful design and meticulous maintenance. The 
Industrial Revolution began in England during the eighteenth century, completely 
transforming manufacturing processes and technology.
