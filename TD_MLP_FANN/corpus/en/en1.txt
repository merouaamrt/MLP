England is a fascinating country with an incredibly rich history and diverse cultural 
heritage spanning many centuries of development. London, the bustling capital city, is 
home to numerous famous landmarks such as the historic Tower of London, magnificent 
Buckingham Palace, iconic Big Ben and the world-renowned British Museum. The English 
language has become the global lingua franca for international communication, business 
transactions and scientific research worldwide. English literature includes many renowned 
authors like William Shakespeare, Jane Austen, Charles Dickens, George Orwell and 
Virginia Woolf. Traditional English cuisine features distinctive dishes like fish and 
chips, roast beef with Yorkshire pudding, shepherd's pie and the classic afternoon tea 
with scones, jam and clotted cream.
